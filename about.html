About Me

This is who I am and what I believe in.


SEUNGHYUN BANG

I am a proud son, a proud returned missionary, and a proud veteran of the Republic of Korea Army. I believe in making the world a better place.

I was born and raised in a city called Incheon, South Korea. Fifteen years ago, when I was in the elementary school, I had a classmate who had disabilities. Back then, fair treatment for people with disabilities was not a familiar concept. Watching him struggle with school work, I began to wonder; "what if people with disabilities can receive fair treatment?"

I believe fairness can be achieved with a simple idea, "Everyone is equal." You, me, and the random people you meet, are all beloved sons and daughters. Just like you deserve good education and career, people with disabilities deserve good life.

Through many years of service for the community, including LDS missionary work and military service, I solidified my passion for making the world a fair place.

Here is a video about disability awareness. Your love and attention can change the world. Let's work together to make a world a better place.
